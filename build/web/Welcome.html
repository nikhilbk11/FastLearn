Fast Learn Inc.
 

Fast Learn, Inc. is a distance-learning  institute that was founded by Samuel Rogers 10 years ago. 
The annual turnover of the institute is $3 million. 
Fast Learn is known for its exellence in education, attracting thousands of students from various countries.

Disciplines

The institute imparts education in the following disciplines:
  • Arts and Commerce.
  • Management.
  • Information Technology.
  • Foreign Languages.

Location

Fast Learn is one of the most established distance-learning institute in the US with its head office located in New York and several branch offices located across the world.
